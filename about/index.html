About

Betanarratives is a website of digital storytelling. We are interested in “new ways of telling old stories”. This means we like reading, creating and publishing stories which are inventive in both their content and the way they utilize our online platform. Read our most recent story here.

We are a proud publishing affiliate of Melbourne-based publishing organization, The Lifted Brow. We thank them for their continual help and support.
