Collective

Jessica Yu is the editor-in-chief of Betanarratives. Her poetry, fiction, memoir and commentary has been published (or is forthcoming) in The Best Australian Poems 2014, The Lifted Brow (online), Kill Your Darlings, Desktop, Seizure, the Meanjin blog and more. She writes a monthly Faith and Religion column for Right Now.
Francesca Ohlert is deputy editor at Betanarratives. She is a subeditor at Farrago and deputy editor of The Suburban Review. She is currently completing her Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in English at The University of Melbourne.
Brendan McDougall is subeditor at Betanarratives. His poetry and short fiction has been published in Overland, Voiceworks, Verandah and others. He is a past columnist and subeditor at Farrago.
Angeline Armstrong is video producer at Betanarratives. She is a media artist, filmmaker and screenwriter. She completed a mentorship with Baz Luhrmann in 2013 and has been taught by staff at RMIT, NIDA & AFTR.
Meg Gough-Brooks is art editor at Betanarratives. She is a curator, visual artist and animator. In 2014, she exhibited in a group show, SOFT, at Fort Delta gallery for the Melbourne Fringe Festival. She has worked on commission for the City of Melbourne, and her multimedia animation, House, was featured in the Signal Screen installation in 2013.
MeiMei Xu is graphic designer at Betanarratives. She is an emerging fashion designer. Her womenswear collection, Legrete has been featured on PITCH Presents and is forthcoming in METAL magazine. Her fashion film, STIUS was exhibited at the NGV as part of the Jean Paul Gautier Open Studio Exhibition.
James Cocciardi is game designer at Betanarratives . He has completed a Bachelor of Interactive Entertainment majoring in Games Design at Qantam College, as well as an advanced diploma in ICT.
Mellisa Hankins is multimedia designer of Betanarratives. She previously studied Multimedia Systems & Design at RMIT, specialising in design and programmin, as well as a Certificate III in 3D Animation & Game Development at AIE.
Bradley Jackson is web designer at Betanarratives. He is a computer programmer in the final year of his Bachelor of Science (Computing and Information Systems) at The University of Melbourne. In the past he has developed applications on Android, and has worked with Ruby On Rails and Django for web development.
Erin Leeder is social media manager at Betanarratives. She is in the final year of her journalism degree at La Trobe University and has been a staff writer for Upstart. She also manages the social media profiles of aid organisation, Seven Women.
