Collaborate

If you curate, write, design, shoot, edit, animate, illustrate, code or basically create anything that you think would fit in with us then send us a pitch here. We’re happy to collaborate with anyone who has a bright idea, whether you’re emerging or established, an individual or a collective, hip to the bone or a bit of a dag (guess which one we are).

Keep in mind that we are particularly interested in:

  1. innovative content and fresh ways of telling stories
  2. stories or work which wouldn’t work in a print publication

View our most recent story here to get a sense for the kind of work we like to publish.

If you’re still a little unsure of whether your work suits us then send us a query, here.

We pay all contributors.
